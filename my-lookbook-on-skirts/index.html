My Lookbook on Skirts

`

We all know that white t shirt, blue jeans and LBD (Little Black Dress) are something every woman must have in her wardrobe. But don't forget to have skirts too in your wardrobe. Skirts always give you feminine feel and look whether you go for flared skirt or denim skirt.

Skirts come in all shapes,sizes and cuttings. So difficult task is to find best skirt which suits you and compliments your body type.

Here are my personal favorite skirts from my wardrobe.

1.Pencil Skirt:

Whenever I am in doubt about what to wear,I wear pencil skirt. No matter what mood I have, I always feel beautiful and confident in pencil skirt. This black and white skirt is of Splash. I paired it with sleeveless black top with little sequin work on it. This is a perfect party outfit. I completed my outfit with Catwalk heels

.

2. A-line skirt:

A-line skirts must be everyone's favorite as these are not too long and not too short and just perfect for dinner night. These skirts also compliments everyone's body.

This is my favorite as I have a big obsession of floral patterns. I paired this skirt with solid black crop top. Both skirt and top bought from Singapore Bugis street market. And I always wear my statement neck piece with it.

3. Flared skirt:

If you have those unduly and unwanted bulges then these skirts are perfect to hide those and give you a smart,stylish and girl next door look.

I have this navy blue plain flared skirt from Westside which I paired with soft baby pink striped crop top again from Westside. This casual look is perfect for any college going girl. I completed my look with pointed ballet shoes from Street Style Store.

4.Mini Skirt:

Mini skirts are best outfit for your girl's day out in summer. It gives you smart,gorgeous look and help you beat the heat in summer.

I have this mini skirt from Ginger Lifestyle with all feminine colors on it. I wore this with sheer Lace blue top from Forever 21 .

5.Denim Pencil skirt:

This is my DIY denim skirt at home. I didn't have denim pencil skirt and I couldn't find any good one in stores as well,so decided to cut my husband's old jeans and made this skirt out of it. I wore this skirt with bell sleeves striped top from Shoppers Stop and beige heels from Centro.

6.Denim short skirt:

I think ,every woman must own a denim short skirt as it gives you funky teenager look. These are trendy,cute yet comfortable.

I have this denim short skirt with floral embroidery on it from Forever 21. I paired it with pastel green top from Shoppers Stop. This is off shoulder top but wore it normally on this skirt and teamed this outfit with casual white sneakers.

7. Skorts:

These are skirts with shorts underneath from Ginger Lifestyle. These are cute and most comfortable one.

I wore this skort with plain black 3/4th sleeves top from Code Lifestyle. I don't like to wear prints on prints. So if one is printed I prefer to wear another simple and plain.

So,that's all. These are some of my skirts which I often wear for various occasions. Do let me know which one you like the most. I hope you guys have a great week ahead. XOXO!!

Priyanka
