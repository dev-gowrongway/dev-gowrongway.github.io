My Experience: Balayage Hair Coloring at Home

by Priyanka

2018-08-27

Hair highlights, Balayage changes your look dramatically. It is a great way to refresh your appearance and try something new. Whether you opt coloring all over or highlights or balayage, it will accentuate the beauty of your hair.

Every now n then, I get bored with my hair look. So I always look for something new to try on my hair. I have tried highlights thrice. And I really liked my look.

April this year, I again started looking for something new to change my look as I was going to attend 2-3 family functions in Pune. So I went to a very popular salon in Kothrud area Pune. And I spend quite a good amount just to highlight my hair. And I was so upset with the result. Anyone could hardly see any difference on my hair. I was not at all satisfied with my so-called new look. And after 2 months it almost faded away.

So after this experience,I decided to color my hair at home by myself because I didn't want to spend same amount again in just 2 months. I searched umpteen number of sites,videos from how to choose hair color according to your hair and skin tone to how to apply hair color at home. I gathered all the courage and ordered Revlon Colorsilk beautiful hair color (Medium Blonde No-74) from Amazon.

This is absolutely ammonia free product. It comes with ammonia free Colorant, developer,after-color conditioner,plastic gloves and instruction leaflet.

I wanted balayage look for my hair. I applied the color and kept it for almost 1.15 hrs. I used broad tooth vented hair brush and hair coloring brush for application. After wash,my hair got lighten but not as per my expectations. So again I applied color which was left after first application and kept it for 1 hr. After second wash,there was a dramatic change in my hair color. And I was so relieved and happy with the result. Let me tell you this,my hair is jet black in color. So it's quit difficult to lighten my hair without bleach.

Some pointers based on my experience:

  1. Go for ammonia free product.
  2. First decide what color you want for your look,check your skin tone,natural hair color and accordingly purchase product. And for this,don't trust on the picture shown on hair color box. You will never get same color as shown on the box.
  3. If you have a deep black hair like me,go literally for blonde shades. Blonde shade gives you at least shade closer to brown or light brown. Else Red or Burgundy gives you nice shade.
  4. Likewise choose color carefully by referring shade card.
  5. Do a patch test before using color on your skin.
  6. Dont apply color on roots for highlights or balayage look. Start coloring by leaving 3-4 inches from roots.
  7. Balayage is a free-form method of hair coloring. You don't need aluminium foil for this,you just need to apply color in a sweeping motion.
  8. The Balayage sweeps should be very close and soft leading to a thicker highlights at the ends of the hair.

My hair look before coloring but with slight highlights from previous salon visit.

So this is my final look. I have done this almost 2 months back. Hair color always look different and lighter in natural light. So test your experiment in natural light.

That’s about it for my hair update. I hope you guys enjoyed and it gave you some idea if you’re wanting to change your look! Talk to you very soon. Good Day!

Disclaimer:
Above write-up and tips are totally based on my personal experience. I am not a professional hair stylist. But I like to try everything by myself so I tried this at home. I suggest you to search thoroughly before doing this at home.
