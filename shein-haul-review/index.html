Shein Haul and Review

Hello lovelies,

I am back again and this time with my Shein shopping and its review.

Who doesn't like to shop online? Myntra, Amazon, Flipkart are really popular online shopping websites for Indians. But when it comes to fashion, it becomes quite difficult to shop online as we need to be really careful about size and material.

Have you ever thought about making a purchase from Shein?? Shein is really making it's name big among girls these days. Those who don't know what Shein is, let me introduce you to it.

What is Shein?
It is a fashion website which offers a wide variety of trendy clothing at affordable rate. So for my birthday, I decided to order something from Shein as the dresses and accessories on Shein always attracts me. So almost a week before my birthday, I placed an order.

So without wasting any time, lets have a look on my products from Shein.

What I bought?

  1. Yellow cut and sew dress:
     I was looking for some casual dresses so I bought this bright yellow color casual dress in just Rs. 595. I must tell you, material and quality is far better than my expectations, especially at this price.

2. Scallop laser cut dress:
This one is also casual dress. I just loved the color of this dress so I bought this without having second thought and bought it at Rs. 390. It fits well and difficult to find such elegant dress at this price.

3. Bow Detail Flounce Sleeve Keyhole Back Blouse:
This is simple yet beautiful top. These flounce sleeves are so in fashion these days. I bought this at just Rs. 495 and it fits as per my likeness. Material is very soft and it is a perfect winter top for me (though in Hyderabad, climate is never so cold).

4. Gold Plated Suede Cord Drop Earrings:
Got these in just Rs. 130 to wear on the yellow dress and it looks cool with the dress. In addition, it doesn't look cheap and has good built quality.

5.  Faux Pearl Decorated Half Circle Stud Earrings:
I paired these with maroon top. These earrings are amazingly beautiful. These are also of Rs. 130. I bet you won't get such good quality in this amount.

6. Feather & Leaf Embellished Drop Earrings:
I bought these to pair it with blue dress in the same amount at Rs. 130. They totally exceeded my expectations as these looks beautiful with the dress.

7.  Faux Pearl Detail Long Drop Earrings:
I am in love with these earrings. These are super elegant,classy and of amazing quality at just Rs. 60. Cant wait to wear them!

How to select size of products?
Selecting a size is dilemma when it comes to online shopping, same goes with Shein. Trick part about buying on Shein- you might need to buy different sizes for different products like my dresses are of size L but top is of size M. So you need to check sizing details of every product as they are totally different. So go and get your measuring tape. It will help a ton to decide your correct size.

Reviews are your friend:
Reading reviews from actual buyers on Shein is the most important thing. It is a great help to decide purchasing the product as you will get an idea about size, material, quality and packaging. I never buy anything before reading it's reviews. Reviews at Shein render authentic and are pretty spot on.

About delivery of the product:
Quoted delivery of my producst was 2 to 3 weeks. But to my surprise, they delivered the products within a week. I was lucky to got my products delivered so early. But it is not the case always. You need to be really very patient if you want to buy from Shein. Because as per the reviews on internet, Shein take at least 2 weeks for delivery.

Quality of the product:
I ordered the products from Shein without any expectations. But quality of each product really surprised me and they are beautiful and amazing. I am totally satisfied and happy with my shopping experience with Shein.

What to buy from Shein:
Shein has really nice and chic collection of dresses. You must check out their dresses collection. And I bet you can't resist ordering them.
Also, it has a beautiful and big collection of accessories and that too at great price!

My overall experience:
I am really very impressed with Shein. The website is very easy to use and user friendly. They have an app, and it's even easier. Multiple payment options are available, but I opted for COD as I was shopping for the first time on Shein. Almost everyday they have some discounts,so you can bag amazing products at discounted price.
P.S.- I bought during offers.

Will I buy again from Shein?
I have had a great experience of Shein. Every single item I bought has a good quality,and it fits me well. So definitely I will buy again from Shein rather my cart is already filled with 3-4 more products. So if you are bored of same type of dresses or tops and looking for something trendy just go for Shein.

Shein was something I always wanted to try, so I hope this helps others who are considering buying from them!

Have a great week ahead!XOXO

Priyanka
