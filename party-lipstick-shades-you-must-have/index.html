Let Your Lips Do the Talking

Welcome back to my another blog where I am going to showcase some of the party perfect lipstick shades.
If diamonds are girl's best friend, lipsticks are her soul mate. There are plethora of options in terms of brands, texture, colors in the world of lipsticks.
I am more inclined to dark shades than nudes. 'Pick only one feature at a time for highlight either eyes or lips' is a basic makeup rule. I don't like to wear heavy eye makeup so I always highlight my lips with dark shades.

Without wasting any further time, lets jump into the details.

  1. Smashbox :
    This Smashbox lipstick in the shade Witchy (Deep Burgundy)from the mini lipstick set is my favorite one. These lipsticks are glossy but can be matte by applying Insta Matte liquid on the lips. This one is a perfect party lipstick and I can use this as a glossy or matte. Smashbox has became one of my favorite brands for lipsticks since I started using this mini lipstick set. These are totally smudge proof, long lasting and feels very silky smooth on the lips with decent pigmentation. It has pretty good hydrating formula. I got this set last year during Black Friday sale from Sephora. This was limited edition from Smashbox. So I am not sure if it is still available or not, but Nykaa has Smashbox lipstick collection on their site. You can buy this here.
    Price of set : 25$

2.  NYX Professional :
This one is from the range of NYX Professional Makeup Soft Matte Lip Cream in shade Budapest. This is liquid matte lip cream which is again long lasting and definitely smudge proof. It has super pigmentation, velvety smooth finish and feels light on the lips. One biggest advantage of liquid lipstick is you don't need lip liner. Check out this lipstick here.
Price : Rs. 600

3.  Chambor :
Red Lips are timeless. Its a classic look that can be worn anytime. I have this red liquid lipstick in the shade of 435 Oh My Rouge from Chambor extreme Transferproof Liquid Lipstick collection. This is the darkest shade I have. As the name says, it is absolutely transferproof, but dose not last long and it makes my lips dry. So I have to hydrate my lips nicely before using this lipstick. But still I like this lipstick because of the shade. Buy It here.
Price : Rs. 845

￼4.  Sephora :
This one is from Sephora collection from the range of Cream Lip Stain Liquid Lipstick in the shade 17 Dark Red. This is highly pigmented that only one layer is all you need. It goes on smooth as butter. Even though it has matte finish, it does not dry your lips. It has a good long lasting formula which makes it worth the buy. You can buy this here.
Price : 14 CHF (Bought this from Manor Switzerland)

￼5.  Nykaa :
This one is my recent purchase from the range of Nykaa Matte to Last Liquid Lipstick in the shade Begum. Nykaa has given their lipsticks name keeping a particular city of India in mind. I have read most of the reviews as these lipsticks dry out your lips. But I didn't feel the dryness. It is really smooth on my lips. It is definitely smudge proof but not long lasting. It would lasts upto 4-5 hours. It has a great creamy texture with nice fragrance. Even though it has a matte texture, it shines a bit on lips. Check out the collection here.
Price : Rs. 550

Girls, lipstick can't solve all problems, but its a pretty great start. These are some of my top picks for lipstick. So If you are looking for some deeper,darker shades; you can try these shades and brands.
I hope you like this post. Signing off.
XOXO!!
