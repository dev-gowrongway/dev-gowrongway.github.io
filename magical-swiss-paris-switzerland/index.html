Magical Swiss Paris: Switzerland

`

Last week's post was all about Paris. This time I came with a blog post of Switzerland journey. If you haven't read my Paris journey experience, you can read that here.

Day 4 in Switzerland : Early morning ,we started our road trip to Switzerland. Scenic views and green lush on both the sides of the road made our journey unforgettable. This was our most colorful road trip ever.

Our destination was Basel. We reached there around 6 in the evening. Basel is truly a city of best architecture. It is such a quite,well organized city. It wasn't dark until very late in the night so we took a stroll nearby hotel.

Day 5 in Switzerland : After Swiss breakfast, we started our journey to Grindelwald by coach. Grindelwald is a picture perfect small mountain village in the Switzerland.

We took a cogwheel train from Grindelwald to Kleine scheidegg and from Kleine scheidegg to Jungfroujoch. This journey takes around an hour to reach Jungfroujoch. The view all the way up and down Jungfroujoch is spectacular and picturesque.

Peaks of snow mountains are forever fascinating.

Apart from ice peaks, there are so many other things to do there like visiting museum, walking through ice caves, skiing, experiencing food at famous Indian restaurant "Bollywood". Jungfroujoch was heavenly experience. This is a must visit place in Switzerland.

On our return journey from kleine scheidegg to Basel,we took a stop at Interlaken town. This town is famous for Yash Chopra movies and his bronze statue. This small town village is between the two lakes namely lake Brianze and lake Thun and hence the name Interlaken which means in between the lakes.

It is truly a treat to eyes as it is surrounded by snow mountains and filled with lush greenery and beautiful flowers.

Day 6  in Switzerland : We started this day with a trip to lake Brianze which is located in the east of Interlaken and it gives you a glimpse of Bernese Oberland mountains.

This lake is extra ordinary clean and turquoise in color. It is so clean that no fishes can survive in it. We took one hour spectacular and thrilling jet boat ride in the lake.

Later this day, we traveled to Lucerne city which is known for its old architecture. Lion monument and kapell bridge are some of the attractions of Lucerne city.

Then we took a walk around the shopping area. As I was in Switzerland ,I just couldn't miss a chance to buy some souvenirs, Swiss cow bell and of course world famous Swiss chocolates.

Day 7  in Switzerland : On day 7,we went to see Mount Titlis to experience yet another memorable day. We started from Basel to Engelberg and from there we took a gondola cable car to Trusbee.

And from Trusbee, we took a world's first 360 degree revolving cable car ride to Mount Titlis. It was once in a lifetime experience. We were literally spellbound by seeing beautiful snow mountain peaks.

Ice flyer chair lift, glacier cave, Titlis cliff walk are top attractions on mount Mitlis. On top of this,there is Movenpick Swiss ice cream shop to have lip smacking ice cream in such a cold icy weather.

On our return journey, we took a stop at Rhine Falls. Rhine fall near Schaffhausen on the Swiss-German border is Europe’s largest waterfall. We took a 15 mins boat ride over there and the experience was just amazing.

We directly took a road to Olten on our return journey.

Last day in Switzerland : We wake up early in the morning and we took a morning walk around our hotel area.

We check out from hotel after breakfast and we went to see Lake Zurich. We were fortunate that we got to spend our morning at Lake zurich as the environment there was quite and cool.

We spend our afternoon at Bahnhofstrasse. Bahnhofstrasse is Zurich's main downtown street and one of the world's most expensive and exclusive shopping avenues. Many of the world’s luxury brands such as Jimmy Choo, Prada, Chanel shop houses are there on Bahnhofstrasse. Bahnhofstrasse means “main train street” in German.

It is one of Zurich’s most beautiful streets with many big Swiss banks are located on it. We took a small walk around the street as we have to left for airport for our return flight to India.

This was one of the most memorable trips we had.

I will soon meet you with another exciting blog. Have a great week ahead!

Priyanka
