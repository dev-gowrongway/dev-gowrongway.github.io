About Me

Hi! Welcome to my blog earthlings. I am Priyanka from Pune, India. Marathi mulgi, living in Hyderabad, an IT engineer by education and an artist at the core. I have tried from working in an IT MNC to work as a trainer in an institute. While I was in IT field, I always had thoughts of doing something in field am passionate about.So I bid adieu to my job and recently started my venture of bespoke exquisite artwork. And now my days are filled with colors, art and ideas. I am truly obsessed with shopping, makeup and fashion, be it high-end brands or street shopping. To me, being shopaholic is far better than being alcoholic. My love for art, shopping, fashion encouraged me to start blogging. So here I present my blog with my personal thoughts, tips and tricks about an art, craft, makeup, fashion, lifestyle and food. Hope you all will find my blog helpful and interesting.
